Your Italian Evening Awaits

At Serata Italiana, we don't just serve meals; we craft memories. Step into the warmth of our trattoria and let us transport you to a piazza in Rome, a vineyard in Tuscany, or a coastal village in Sicily—all in one unforgettable night.

La passione per la Vita ~ a passion for life

The Story of a Single Night It was one perfect night in a piazza in Rome. The air was warm, a guitarist was playing, and a simple meal of cacio e pepe and a glass of wine felt like the most luxurious thing in the world. We thought, "Why can't every night feel like this?" That's the question we built Serata Italiana to answer. We are a team of dreamers, nonna's recipe thieves, and wine enthusiasts obsessed with bringing the warmth, charm, and effortless joy of an Italian evening to Nairobi City. We're not about white tablecloths and hushed tones; we're about the vibrant buzz of conversation, the sharing of plates, and the creation of your own perfect night. Come in, take a seat, and let us help you write your story.

Our Menu

At Serata Italiana, our menu is a love letter to Italy's rich culinary heritage. From the rolling hills of Tuscany to the sun-kissed coasts of Sicily, each dish is crafted with passion and authenticity. Whether you're craving a hearty plate of pasta, a fresh seafood delight, or a decadent dessert, our offerings promise to transport your taste buds straight to Italy.
